Composition Tips
  • Enter the options text inside Option 1 to 4 text inputs.
  • Click the circular radio button to the left of the correct option to select it.
  • Assign points weight to set scores (e.g. 1 point for easy questions, 5 points for advanced problems).
  • Once added, questions can be queried inside the "Manage Questions" library.
